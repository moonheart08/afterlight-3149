A smaller vessel with a plethora of medical equipment.
Not capable of self sustaining outside of what it can find on debris,
however, so be prepared to need a hand from your fellows.

Remarks: You start with chemicals, which are rare on the Rim. Use these
to your advantage, whether it is for healing or trading for goods.
Roster: 1 Chief Medical Officer, 1 Salvage Specialists, 2 Medical Doctors
